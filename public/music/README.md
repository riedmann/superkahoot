# Background Music

Add your background music files here:

## Recommended Files:
- `game-music.mp3` - Main background music (MP3 format)
- `game-music.ogg` - Main background music (OGG format for better browser support)

## Music Suggestions:
- Upbeat, energetic background music
- Instrumental tracks work best
- 2-5 minute loops
- Medium volume tracks that won't interfere with speech

## Free Music Sources:
- [Freesound.org](https://freesound.org) - Creative Commons licensed
- [Free Music Archive](https://freemusicarchive.org) - Public domain and CC
- [Zapsplat](https://zapsplat.com) - Free with account
- [YouTube Audio Library](https://studio.youtube.com/channel/UCuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuu/music) - Royalty-free

## Format Support:
- MP3 (most compatible)
- OGG (good quality, open source)
- WAV (large files, high quality)

The game will automatically loop the music and allow hosts to start/stop playback during the game.
